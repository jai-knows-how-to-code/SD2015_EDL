About

Extreme Design labs - A story of 3 young dynamic aspiring individuals look to change the world

  • 2014

    Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute

    Jai, Mithun and Nachiket were accepted into the MS IT program at RPI and started school together during the Fall of 2015.The uniting force behind them was Professor Roger Grice

  • August 2014

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Extreme Design labs was started as UI Geeks back in Fall 2014 along with their classmate Jake Piao and Rahul Kumar at their User Experience Design class. The 5 of them got together with an aim of redesigning the questionable systems in the world. They started off with re-designing RPI highly questionable system - SIS.

  • January 2015

    An Agency is Born

    The team decided to leverage their design experience and technical skillset to move into design and development of web applications. Extreme Design labs came into existence in the Studio Design in Human Computer Interaction Class. The team without Jake Piao, set out to build InstaRent a rent payment and property management tool.

  • August 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    Empowered by their internship experiences at Blacstone, Bank of America and Amazon. Jai,Mithun and Nachiket decided to move Extreme Design labs to a botique full service consulting and technology services firm.

  • 2015-2016

    Phase Two Expansion

    Extreme Design labs is looking to expand to meet the needs of the Mobile landscape.Unfortunately, Only a max of 3 students are allowed to be part of this team for the Software Development class. You can work for us unoffically if you like.
